About UnityPass

UnityPass is a digital platform that allows unskilled and semi-skilled workers to create verified profiles showcasing their abilities. Each profile includes a unique QR code and downloadable ID card, helping workers get recognized and hired easily.

How it works: Register your skill → Generate your profile → Share your QR for instant verification.
