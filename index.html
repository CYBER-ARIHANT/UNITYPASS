About UnityPass

UnityPass is a digital identification and empowerment initiative designed to help unskilled and semi-skilled workers showcase their abilities through a verified online skill ID. It enables individuals to register their skills, generate a QR-based digital card, and share it with potential employers, helping bridge the gap between opportunity and talent.

Anyone can use UnityPass to store their professional details securely and present them digitally — promoting transparency, recognition, and easy access to job and training opportunities.

About Us

Our team — Arihant Jha, Aarav Arora, Prisha Jain, and Shreyasee Raipal — are students of Class 9 who have collaboratively developed UnityPass. This project aims to promote employment and empowerment among unskilled and semi-skilled workers by providing them with a simple, digital platform to register, showcase, and verify their skills.

Through UnityPass, we aspire to bridge the gap between opportunity and talent, helping workers gain recognition for their abilities and enabling easier access to job and training opportunities.
