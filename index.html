What is UnityPass?

UnityPass is a digital skill ID card designed to help individuals showcase their skills professionally. By registering on UnityPass, users can create a digital profile containing personal details, skills, and location. This profile can be shared with employers or training providers, empowering unskilled and semi-skilled workers to access better employment opportunities efficiently.
