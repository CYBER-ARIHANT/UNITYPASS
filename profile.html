About UnityPass

UnityPass is a digital skill ID card designed to help individuals document and showcase their skills in a professional manner. By registering on UnityPass, users can create a digital profile containing their personal details, skills, and location. This profile can be shared with employers or training providers, helping unskilled and semi-skilled workers gain access to employment opportunities and skill development programs efficiently and securely.
